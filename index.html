Hall Assassins

Welcome to Hall Assassins, the exciting team-based elimination game for high school students! Register now to join the competition.

Registration Details

  • First round will begin Monday, April 21st.
  • Payments will be due Friday, April 18th at 11:59 PM.
  • After your payment is confirmed, please DM this account: Provide your First Name, Last Name, and Grade as well as your partner's First Name, Last Name, and Grade. (Only one partner DM)

General Game Rules

  • Teams of two.
  • First round will begin Monday, April 21st at 6:00 AM.
  • Assignments will be given Sunday, April 20th.
  • Each assignment will be given the day prior to the start of the next round.
  • Each round will last between 3–5 days (announcements will be made on Instagram).
  • If you attract the attention of police or administration, your team is out.
  • You may not enter anyone’s home unless invited in by someone who lives there.

Safe Zones

  • School Grounds (including Karen Road, Hyde/Maiden & the Eisenhower parking lot) are safe from 5 a.m. until 30 minutes after all school events are finished at night.
  • Cars are NOT safe! If it is unlocked, you may enter. You cannot break into a car if it is locked.
  • Club and school sports are safe until you enter your car and close the door to leave.
  • Workplaces and their parking lots are safe if you are working and until you enter your car and close the door to leave.
  • Religious services and their parking lots are safe until you enter your car and close the door to leave.
  • Doctor’s appointments, hospitals and their parking lots are safe until you enter your car and close the door to leave.
  • School sponsored events and their parking lots are safe until you enter your car and close the door to leave.
  • Charity events and their parking lots are safe until you enter your car and close the door to leave.
  • Team dinners/Psych parties are safe from the time you arrive until you get in your car and close the door to leave.
  • Team dinners/Psych parties are safe from the time you arrive until you get in your car and close the door to leave.

Additional Rules

  • Bus stops are considered safe zones, but the walk to the bus in the morning and back to your house after school are considered fair game.
  • There will be a hiatus in the game during prom night/beach weekend (more information later).
  • Buying back in will be allowed until the third round (DOUBLE original buy in).
  • The team that wins will be allotted a cash prize.
  • Juniors who participate will be playing for the opportunity to run the game next year.
  • The team of Juniors that advances the furthest will be the two people to run the game the following year.
